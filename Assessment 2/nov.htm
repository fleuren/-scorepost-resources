Gameplay

The novice difficulty consists of charts with
primarily easy songs. Things such as filler
notes, simple rhythms, and repetitive easy patterns
are included in this difficulty set.

Its later stages serve as practice for the easier
segments of the Medium/Advanced difficulty.
